Transparent by default

Every call you make is public. Every resolution is public. There is no edit-after, no quiet delete. The record is the record.

A publicly-acknowledged miss adds to Intellectual Honesty — sometimes more than a quiet hit adds to Accuracy. The institution rewards honesty.
